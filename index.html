Mission

We use technology to bring a better future to the present.

Spend 10 minutes a day to make the world a better place

Rome wasn’t built in a day, and the free speech movement didn’t take place overnight. However, big changes are made up of small ones — and we believe that if all of us can spend just 10 minutes a day improving something in our own world, then we are coming together for a larger purpose. No one can take care of all 7 billion people on earth, but if everyone can take care of the few hundred who matter to them, then all 7 billion people are being taken care of.

Big data doesn't have to be so far away

Many shy away from large chunks of data because they come in confusing formats, often with strange abbreviated variable names. They are formidable blocks of text staring at you through the screen, but it doesn't have to be that way. We use modern technology to serve as a catalyst for the skills of critical thinking that have allowed humans to thrive. By making big data more accessible and easier to navigate, we are making it easier than ever for you to form your own opinions based on data free of bias.
