About Us

Founded in 2015 by sociology Ph.D. Nick Adams, Goodly Labs is an organization dedicated to social science research and education. Our goal is to create and share technological skills that will help people open discussions with one another peacefully and effectively.

As our work is centralized around bringing information to people, transparency is one of our strongest values. We share our plans so that everyone can understand the steps we are taking to build a better future. Through this, we are a community fighting for a common vision of what a collaborative society could look like. Our teams are as driven as they are intelligent, and the work that they do is open to anyone who asks for educational purposes.

Goodly welcomes participants of all backgrounds, and we pride ourselves on the diversity of our members. People come from a wide set of income levels, educational backgrounds, and ethnicities. To be a part of our family, please visit our Join Us page and consider donating or becoming a board member.
